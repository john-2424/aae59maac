•	Course Projects (55 Points): Students are also required to complete a course project individually. The project topic will be selected by students through a broad reading of the literature in multi-agent systems.  For each project, one mid-term report (20 points) including a clear problem formulation and introduction need to be submitted no later than 11:59pm on March 13, 2025. A final report (35 points) in the form of technical paper must be submitted by 11:59pm on May 4, 2025. The final report must be in the form of a technical paper, which needs to include Motivation, Problem Formulation, Key Idea, Main Results, Simulations (or Proofs).
